Realm of the Mad God

Realm of the Mad God also known as RotMG is a massively co-op fantasy adventure game.
It is a retro-style top down 2d game that has origins as an indie game
that got 3rd place in the TIGSource Assemblee Game Contest.

The game is available to play on Steam.

Experience

2022 - 2024

To begin with I worked on the game as a Closed Tester at DECA Games and later
became a Future Seeker more commonly known as a User Generated Content (UGC) contributor. UGC is a title granted
to individuals who contribute to the game's development by creating dungeons, items, enemies, maps, and other in-game assets.

After two years as a Closed Tester, I transitioned into UGC work, where I began designing a dungeon
for the game. While this project remained unreleased due to personal time constraints, it was
developed to an advanced stage and showcases my ability to conceptualize and implement
engaging game design ideas. During this process I worked with talented artists who are
now part of the official team behind the game working as UGC too.

My Dungeon Design

The unreleased dungeon I worked on was inspired by classic psychological horror titles like Silent Hill and Resident Evil.
This nightmare-themed concept aimed to bring unique mechanics and atmospheric storytelling to Realm of the Mad God.
Set within the traumatized mind of a player's character haunted by endless battles against Oryx's monstrous hordes,
the dungeon embodied a state of chaotic despair and an establishment of a first person narrative for the first time in the game.
It was designed as a heavily solo-oriented experience, featuring:

* Intense bullet-hell shot patterns, ramping up difficulty and precision requirements.
* A completely blacked-out minimap, heightening the disorienting and unsettling ambiance.

These mechanics, paired with the thematic focus, aimed to immerse players in a surreal nightmare
while introducing innovative gameplay elements to the game's dungeon roster.
A majority of the artwork seen throughout the dungeon was made by Dappertron & Outerwar.

Dungeon Name

Unlike every other dungeon in the game, this one does not have
a name, hinting to the player that it is not actually a dungeon
in the physical world within the RotMG universe.

Conclusion

This project, while unreleased, reflects the creative and technical process involved in UGC work for
Realm of the Mad God. The development and submission of UGC content follow a rigorous pipeline:
gathering feedback, iterative testing, and eventual review by DECA Games' designers to ensure
alignment with the game's quality standards.

Although this dungeon never reached the stage of submission for DECA's review due to personal reasons,
it remains an accomplishment I am proud of, representing both my design skills and dedication. This page
documents the current state of the project and serves as a foundation for potential future work
whether with DECA Games or as part of broader discussions on dungeon design in similar games.

My plan is to eventually revisit this topic of game design for ingame dungeons.
